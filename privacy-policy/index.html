Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026
At FRKN, your privacy is our priority. We collect only the minimum amount of data required to provide and maintain a high-performance, secure connection.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following limited information:

  • Personal Information: Your email address (if provided during registration or trial access request).
  • Unique Identifier: A system-generated ID used to authenticate your subscription and manage service access.
  • Technical Data: Device type, app version, and basic connectivity logs (required for technical functionality and load balancing).

We do not collect sensitive data such as contacts, photos, browser history, or precise geolocation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Providing and maintaining the technical infrastructure.
  • Managing user subscriptions and service access.
  • Sending essential service notifications (e.g., trial activation or account updates).
  • Improving service performance and reliability.

3. Data Retention and Sharing

3.1. Retention: We store your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. Connection logs are stored temporarily and are subject to automatic deletion.

3.2. Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may only be shared with infrastructure providers necessary to operate the service or if required by law.

4. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you or request its deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the support channels listed below.

6. Third-Party Services

Our infrastructure may rely on third-party hosting and delivery providers. These partners have their own privacy policies and are chosen for their reliability and security standards.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
