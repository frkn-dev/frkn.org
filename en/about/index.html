What is Reelsoprovod

Reelsoprovod is a service by FRKN Privacy Company that helps maintain fast and stable access to the modern internet.

We believe the internet should work equally well for all users — without artificial restrictions, slowdowns, or unnecessary obstacles.

Reelsoprovod creates a secure connection between your device and the FRKN network. This lets you comfortably use your favorite websites, videos, messengers, neural networks, and online services.

Setup takes less than a minute and works on all popular devices.

What Reelsoprovod is used for

  • Watching high-quality video
  • Messaging in messengers
  • Working with neural networks and cloud services
  • Online gaming
  • Protecting your connection on public Wi-Fi networks
  • Enhancing privacy while browsing the internet

About FRKN and our approach

FRKN is an independent engineering project created by networking and infrastructure specialists.

We develop technologies for security, privacy, and reliable access to information.
