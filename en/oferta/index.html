1.1 General Provisions

This Public Offer contains the terms for concluding the Services Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Services Agreement" and/or "Agreement"). An offer is understood as a proposal addressed to one or several specific persons, which is sufficiently definite and expresses the intention of the person making the proposal to consider itself bound by an agreement with the addressee who accepts the proposal.

The performance of the actions specified in this Offer constitutes confirmation of both Parties' consent to conclude the Services Agreement on the terms, in the manner, and to the extent set forth in this Offer.

The text of this Public Offer below is an official public proposal of the Contractor addressed to interested persons to conclude a Services Agreement in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 2 of Article 437 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.

The Services Agreement shall be deemed concluded and shall enter into force from the moment the Parties perform the actions provided for in this Offer, which signify unconditional and full acceptance of all terms of this Offer without any exceptions or limitations on an accession basis.

1.2 Terms and Definitions

1.2.1 Agreement — the text of this Offer with Appendices that are an integral part hereof, accepted by the Customer through conclusive actions provided for in this Offer.

1.2.2 Conclusive actions — conduct expressing consent to a counterparty's proposal to conclude, amend, or terminate an agreement. Such actions consist of full or partial performance of the conditions proposed by the counterparty.

1.2.3 Contractor's Website on the Internet — a set of computer programs and other information contained in an information system, access to which is provided via the Internet under the domain name and network address: https://frkn.org

1.2.4 Parties to the Agreement — the Contractor and the Customer.

1.2.5 Service — a service provided by the Contractor to the Customer in the manner and on the terms established by this Offer.

2. Subject of the Agreement

2.1 The Contractor undertakes to provide the Customer with the Services, and the Customer undertakes to pay for them in the amount, manner, and within the timeframes established by this Agreement.

2.2 The name, quantity, procedure, and other conditions for the provision of Services are determined based on the Contractor's information when the Customer places an order, or are established on the Contractor's Website on the Internet.

2.3 The Contractor provides Services under this Agreement either personally or with the involvement of third parties, while remaining liable to the Customer for the actions of such third parties as for its own.

2.4 The Agreement is concluded by acceptance of this Offer through conclusive actions expressed in:

  • actions related to registering an account on the Contractor's Website on the Internet, if account registration is required;
  • placing and sending an order by the Customer to the Contractor for the provision of Services;
  • actions related to payment for Services by the Customer;
  • actions related to the provision of Services by the Contractor.
This list is not exhaustive; other actions that clearly express a person's intention to accept a counterparty's proposal are also possible.

3. Rights and Obligations of the Parties

3.1 Rights and Obligations of the Contractor:

3.1.1 The Contractor undertakes to provide the Services in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement, within the timeframes and to the extent specified in this Agreement and/or in the manner specified on the Contractor's Website.

3.1.2 The Contractor undertakes to provide the Customer with access to the sections of the Website necessary to obtain information in accordance with clause 2.1 of the Agreement.

3.1.3 The Contractor is responsible for the storage and processing of the Customer's personal data, ensures the confidentiality of such data, and uses it solely for the quality provision of Services to the Customer.

3.1.4 The Contractor reserves the right to unilaterally change the timeframes (period) for the provision of Services and the terms of this Offer without prior notice to the Customer by publishing such changes on the Contractor's Website on the Internet. In this case, the new/amended terms apply only to newly concluded Agreements.

3.2 Rights and Obligations of the Customer:

3.2.1 The Customer is obliged to provide accurate information about themselves when receiving the relevant Services.

3.2.2 The Customer undertakes not to reproduce, duplicate, copy, sell, or use in any way the information and materials for purposes other than personal use without granting access to third parties.

3.2.3 The Customer undertakes to accept the Services provided by the Contractor.

3.2.4 The Customer is entitled to demand a refund exclusively on the grounds provided for by the applicable legislation of the Russian Federation.

3.2.5 The Customer warrants that all terms of the Agreement are clear to them; the Customer accepts the terms without reservations.

3.2.6 The Customer undertakes not to use the Service for actions that violate the legislation of the Russian Federation, and to bear full and sole responsibility for any consequences arising from such use.

4. Price and Payment Procedure

4.1 The cost of the Contractor's Services and the procedure for their payment are determined when the Customer places an order or are established on the Website: https://frkn.org

4.2 All settlements under the Agreement are made in a non-cash manner.

5. Confidentiality and Security

5.1 The Parties ensure confidentiality and data security in accordance with Federal Law No. 152-FZ and Federal Law No. 149-FZ.

5.2 The Parties undertake to keep confidential the information received during the performance of the Agreement.

5.3 Confidential information means any information transmitted by the Parties in the process of implementing the Agreement.

6. Force Majeure

6.1 The Parties are released from liability due to force majeure: prohibitive actions of authorities, natural disasters, etc.

6.2 In the event of such circumstances, the Party is obliged to notify the other Party within 30 business days.

7. Liability of the Parties

7.1 The Parties are liable in accordance with the terms of this Offer.

7.2 The Contractor is not liable if non-performance occurred due to the Customer's fault.
