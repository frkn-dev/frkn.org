Referral Program

Invite friends to FRKN and get bonus days added to your subscription when a friend activates a key for the first time.

Bonus days

The bonus size depends on how many days your friend activates:

Days paid Bonus
30 7 days
90 14 days
365 30 days

For example, if your friend activates 180 days, the bonus will match the nearest lower tier, which is 14 days.

How it works

  1. Copy your referral code from your profile or subscription page.
  2. Share the code with a friend. They enter it during sign-up or in their subscription page.
  3. Your friend signs up and gets a standard trial period to try the VPN for free.
  4. When your friend activates a key for the first time, both of you receive bonus days according to the table above. The bonus days are added to your current subscription term.

What to keep in mind

  • The bonus is one-time. You and your friend receive bonus days according to the table only for their first key activation; the days are added to your current subscription term. Subsequent key activations by the same friend do not grant bonuses.
  • No instant signup bonuses. We do not add days just for entering a code — only for an actual key activation, to prevent abuse.
  • Self-referral is not allowed. You cannot use your own referral code.
  • Chains work. If your friend also invites someone with their own code, your friend receives a bonus as the referrer. You do not participate in that chain — bonuses only apply between the inviter and the invited user.

Where to find your code

Your referral code is located in your profile or on the subscription page. Copy it and share it with friends however you like.
