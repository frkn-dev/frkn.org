# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our application and website.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Personal Information

* Email address (if provided by the user)

### Usage Data

We may collect limited technical data such as:

* Device type
* App version
* Connection logs (for service functionality)

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data such as contacts, photos, or precise location.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

* Provide and maintain the service
* Create and manage user subscriptions
* Send service-related emails (e.g. trial access)
* Improve performance and reliability
* Provide user support

---

## 3. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service.

* Email and account-related data may be stored for service access
* Logs are stored temporarily and may be deleted automatically

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not sell or rent** your personal data.

We may share data only in the following cases:

* To comply with legal obligations
* To protect the service from abuse or fraud
* With infrastructure providers strictly required to operate the service

---

## 5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* Request access to your data
* Request deletion of your data
* Contact us for any privacy-related concerns

## 7. Third-Party Services
